Why a cooperative?

Employees are seeing the adoption of AI technologies as threat, They know that there is a wave of firings because of AI gains in productivity. And no-one likes to train or develop their replacement. The people that are happy with the economic efficiencies of AI are the owners of the company. But in a cooperative the employees are also the owners. So it is in a cooperative's best interest for members to increase productivity with AI. Because they are also participants in the economic benefits of AI.

That is also why an AI native cooperative is better. Because other coops and other companies are currently thinking about downsizing. But a coop that is born with AI efficiencies already taken into account is going to be small from origin. No need to share profits with more people than necessary.

Since October, 2022 when ChatGPT and later Claude came into the market, software companies have been claiming that age of programming is over.
